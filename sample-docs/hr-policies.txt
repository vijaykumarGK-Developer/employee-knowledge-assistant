Employee Handbook — HR Policies

1. Work Hours
   Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday,
   with a one-hour lunch break. Remote work is allowed on Wednesdays
   and Fridays with manager approval.

2. Leave Policy
   Employees receive 24 paid leave days per year, plus 12 public holidays.
   Sick leave is 10 days per year with a medical certificate required for
   absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

3. Probation Period
   New employees undergo a 6-month probation period. Performance reviews
   are conducted at the 3-month and 6-month marks.

4. Benefits
   Health insurance covers employee and dependents. Annual training budget
   of $2,000 per employee. Company contributes 5% to retirement fund.

5. Code of Conduct
   All employees must complete the annual ethics training. Conflicts of
   interest must be disclosed in writing to HR within 30 days.
