Finance Department — Expense & Reimbursement Policy

1. Travel Expenses
   Economy class flights for trips under 6 hours. Business class for
   international flights over 6 hours. Hotel budget capped at $250/night.
   Daily meal per diem is $75 within the country, $120 internationally.

2. Reimbursement Process
   Submit expense reports within 30 days of the expense date. Attach
   original receipts for amounts over $25. Reports under $500 are
   approved automatically. Amounts over $500 require manager approval.
   Amounts over $5,000 require VP approval.

3. Company Credit Cards
   Department heads may request company credit cards with a $10,000
   monthly limit. Cards must be reconciled by the 5th of each month.
   Personal use of company cards is strictly prohibited.

4. Budget Planning
   Annual budget proposals are due by October 15th. Departments must
   justify any increase over 10% from the previous year. Quarterly
   reviews track actual vs. planned spending.

5. Vendor Payments
   Net-30 payment terms for all vendors. Invoices over $50,000 require
   dual signature approval. All vendor contracts must be reviewed by
   the legal team before signing.
